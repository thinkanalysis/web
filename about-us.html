About Us

ThinkAnalysis Inc. is a Toronto-based consulting firm specializing in enterprise IT delivery management. We work with Canadian businesses—particularly in financial services and wealth management—to drive critical initiatives from strategy through execution.

Our strength lies in bridging the gap between vision and delivery. We partner with CIOs, CTOs, and senior IT leaders to bring structure, clarity, and momentum to complex projects. Whether leading strategic planning efforts, stabilizing troubled initiatives, or overseeing multi-team execution, our role is to ensure outcomes are aligned, risks are managed, and delivery stays on track.

ThinkAnalysis is often engaged to provide structure where delivery has stalled, act as a trusted advisor to IT leaders, and oversee the implementation of enterprise-critical capabilities—such as process orchestration, secure identity platforms, data integration frameworks, and cloud-native architectures. We’re known for cutting through ambiguity, aligning cross-functional teams, and delivering results—without unnecessary overhead.

Our approach is lean, direct, and grounded in enterprise reality. We don’t just consult—we lead with purpose.
